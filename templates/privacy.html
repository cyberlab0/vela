Privacy Policy for VELA

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

VELA collects information necessary to provide our AI financial assistant services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address provided during registration.
  • Financial Data: Bank account balances and transaction history synced via our secure gateways strictly for the purpose of personal financial tracking.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the VELA application.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the VELA service.
  • Enable the AI assistant to analyze and summarize your financial data.
  • Send SMS notifications and alerts regarding your account limits.
  • Improve the security and functionality of the app.

3. Data Security & Encryption

Security is our highest priority. VELA uses bank-level 256-bit encryption. All personal and financial data is encrypted at rest and in transit. VELA's AI engine processes data ephemerally and does not use your personal financial data to train public models.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with trusted third-party services (such as our SMS routing and Bank connection partners) strictly necessary to operate the application, and all such partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you, request corrections, or request the complete deletion of your account and associated data. You can exercise these rights by contacting our support team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the VELA application.
